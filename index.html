Explore the Most Beautiful Sites of Morocco

Ourika Valley

Spectacular waterfalls and Berber villages at the foot of the Atlas. A refreshing day trip 1h from Marrakech.

Full day 65 km

Essaouira

UNESCO-listed fortified coastal town. Beautiful beaches, authentic medina and unique maritime atmosphere.

Full day 175 km

Ouzoud Waterfalls

The highest waterfalls in North Africa (110m). Meet the Barbary macaques and enjoy a refreshing swim.

Full day 150 km

Ouarzazate

The "Gateway to the Desert" and Morocco's cinema capital. Majestic kasbahs and stunning desert landscapes.

2 days / 1 night 196 km

Casablanca

Morocco's economic capital. Hassan II Mosque, ocean corniche and Art Deco architecture.

Full day 240 km

Agadir

Modern beach resort with 300 days of sunshine per year. Golden sandy beaches and relaxing atmosphere.

2-3 days 254 km

Agafay Desert

Desert experience 30 minutes from Marrakech. Dinner under the stars and magical sunset.
